OUR ACTIVITY

‘Abhigyata’ is derived from a ‘hindi word’, meaning, ‘Awareness’. It is a non profit initiative started by 2 teenagers
with the intent to impart education and awareness to high school students on organ donation
so as to encourage discussion with their family members to create long term change in their outlook towards deceased organ donation .

ABHIGYATA’S VISION

To offer the right information about organ donation to the youth who can then share the acquired knowledge with all.

ABHIGYATA’S MISSION

Despite the efforts of several organizations to increase awareness, the topic of organ donation is still not discussed openly in Indian families and until this happens the multiplying impact of dissemination of information from one person to another will not occur.
Abhigyata is on a mission to achieve an increase in deceased organ donation so that no one needs to endanger his/ her life by becoming a living donor.

WHAT DO WE DO?

We have designed a project for organ donation awareness by the high school students for the high school students and thus for whole society. Organ donation education is provided by a 30-min long presentation followed by questions and answers to address participants' queries. The presentation imparts knowledge about various aspects of deceased organ donation, information about organ donation and transplantation, difference between living and deceased organ donation, brain stem death and its certification, myths clearing and organ donor pledge registration process. Real stories of recipients and donors are shown. A post presentation questionnaire is conducted to understand the pre and post knowledge and attitude of students. A family interaction feedback response is sought from participants.
